My Favorite Murder

My Favorite Murder is a true crime podcast hosted by karen Kilgariff and Georgia Hardstark. The Podcast first started in early 2016, when the two hosts bonded over their odd fascination with the mystery behind murders.
Since it's inception the show has broken podcast download records and ignited an enthusiastic, interactive "Murderino" fan base who came out in drove for their sold-out nationwide tour.

THE YEARS

My Favorite Murder episodes are released every Thursday, therefore, there are no seasons.
Thus the episodes are presented to you by the years they came out.
They do have Minisodes that are released every Monday, those episodes are not included below.
Occassionally live episodes replace weekly releases, live episodes are not included below.

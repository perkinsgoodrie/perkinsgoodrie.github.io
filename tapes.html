The Black Tapes

The Black Tapes is a podcast from Pacific Northwest Stories and Minnow Beats Whale, and is hosted by Alex Reagan.
It is a serialized docudrama about one journalist's search for the truth, her enigmatic subject's mysterious past, and the literal and fugirative ghosts that haunt them both.

How do you feel about paranormal activity or the Supernatural? Ghosts? Spirits? Demons?

Do you believe?

episode one

A Tale of Two Tapes Part I

In the first episode of The Black Tapes, our host, Alex Reagan, enters the strange world of paranormal investigation and encounters the enigmatic Dr. Richard Strand: a ghost hunter who doesn't believe in ghosts. We follow Alex as she attempts to unlock both the secrets of Strand's collection of mysterious black VHS cases, and the dark and mysterious past of the enigmatic man himself. This is The Black Tapes. Do you believe?
